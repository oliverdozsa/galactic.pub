This is the account that will fund the voting. Upon creating the voting the estimated cost of it will be deducted from this account. When the voting ends, surplus funds will be transferred back to here. DO NOT USE YOUR OWN PRIVATE ACCOUNT HERE! Instead create a new one, and transfer the estimated cost there. You can also choose to use test net if you'd rather not spend any real money first. In this case, you can generate a funding account, and it will be pre-funded.
